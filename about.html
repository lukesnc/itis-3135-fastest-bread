About Us

The Furukawa family is proud to have been baking the fresh bread our customers have come to know and love for over 30 years. Fastest Bread is a family owned business that spans over 2 generations. After giving birth to their daughter Nagisa, Akio and Sanae Furukawa quit their jobs to build Fastest Bread Bakery. Pictured above are the current owners, Nagisa and Tomoya Okazaki with their daughter Ushio.
